About PXO

 

Founded July, 2011
About A community dedicated to the continued support of RPG creation, localization, and comradery. Currently focusing on the Tales Series.

Description

'We' are a group of individuals who love RPGs as a video game genre. Live-action, turn-based, and everything in between; we enjoy role-playing games to the highest degree. Responding to fan enthusiasm, we attempt to persuade the "powers that be" in the video game industry to create RPGs and to localize the ones that never make it to the US/Europe.

Mission

To ensure localization of foreign RPGs to the West, localization itself be better known as a common practice, and to promote the continuation of creation and imagination within the RPG genre.
